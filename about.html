“Every great design begins with an even better story.”

As a UX designer with a background in education/instructional design, I have a knack for gaining an understanding of the people using your products and simplifying designs into intuitive, useful, and attractive products. What does this mean for you? It means employees become more efficient at their jobs, customers experience fewer barriers to accomplishing their goals - and that will either make money or save money. I have experience in instructional design, training, UX design, agriculture, and ceramics. I’m currently located in the Los Angeles area. If you’re looking for someone to deliver experiences that are easy, intuitive, and dare I say delightful for people, let's connect!
